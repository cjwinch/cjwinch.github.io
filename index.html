HUGH

Industrial Liason Officer

EEsoc is one of the most productive and forward thinking student led groups at Imperial.


Though it is students led, the committee is given a lot of support by the department and by our external sponsors.

Companies are only interested in providing us with their time, expertise, and money if they believe that the students in the department have something to offer them.

This is where the industrial liaisons officers come in. We are ambassadors for the department who can are there to maintain links with our current partners and forge new ones, by showcasing the abilities of those in our department.

If elected my goals would be:
  • - To continue the weekly presentations from companies like BP, TFL and Detica.
  • - To bring in even more, exciting and interesting, companies to the already brilliant CarEErs fair.
  • - To improve the number of companies offering 6 month placements to third years.
  • - To help students in all years to get contacts within companies that they are interested in.
I believe that I am one of the correct people for this job for the following reasons:
    - I have had summer work in industry since first year through the Power academy scheme which is supported by many companies of interest.
  • - Talking with company reps at events has already given me links to people in industry.
